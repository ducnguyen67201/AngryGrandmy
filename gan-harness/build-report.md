## Generator/evaluator convergence

### Iteration 1

The generator introduced the central observation prism, persona-attributed in-window cursors, scan sweep, stage telemetry, intentional headline wrapping, and a trajectory-to-evidence signal bridge. The evaluator judged the desktop launch quality strong but failed the pass because reduced motion produced a hydration error, mobile essential text was too small, motion lacked causal hierarchy, the CTA was inert, and the evidence section lost the hero signature.

### Iteration 2

The generator replaced the runtime Motion render layer with stable CSS choreography, kept a stable SSR/client tree, reframed mobile around one readable active browser, reduced simultaneous animation, slowed the trajectory cadence, added an observable accessible panel restart, and refracted prism provenance into the evidence cards. The evaluator confirmed clean normal/reduced browser loads, zero reduced-motion animations, no overflow, functional CTA feedback, improved mobile legibility, and a passing 8.14 score.

## Final evaluator verdict

Credible award-gallery shortlist quality. The interface now communicates the product within the first view, has a GrannySmith-specific prism/trajectory signature, and clears the production bar for hydration, responsive layout, accessibility, motion preferences, and interaction feedback.

## Remaining opportunities

- Make the prism-to-report transformation literal rather than primarily atmospheric.
- Raise remaining mobile persona/status labels to 10–11px.
- Enlarge the desktop active browser and selected observation slightly.
- Freeze inactive route/cursor systems more aggressively late in the sequence.
- Link lower recommendation hover/focus states back to persona evidence.
